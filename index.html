About Me

An ardent creative with a knack for magic behind a text editor, I am the type of player who thrives in a fast-paced environment where results are expected and ingenuity is demanded. I am currently working as a Mentor at DevMountain where I help guide and educate students through their thirteen-week web development immersive bootcamp. Past experience in live entertainment and nightlife taught me crucial skills such as performing under pressure, contributing fully to a team, and preserving a vigorous attitude while maintaining foresight. Web development has become the ideal vehicle for me to satisfy both sides of my brain, putting my talents on display and giving me the opportunity to collaborate with others to create technology that inspires.

PHILOSOPHY

Balance defines my approach to web development and design above anything else. I aim to build an elegant, simple and intuitive UI, while taming any hairiness under the hood down to a non-issue. I am steadfast and stubborn in achieving that balance, because true balance always levels itself out… I have no choice but to see problems through to their eventual solutions.
